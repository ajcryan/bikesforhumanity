We're raising funds to send our next container load of bikes to the Namibia. Each bike costs about $35 to send. So cash donations are essential to help get the bikes to Namibia, administer Bikes for Humanity Sydney, sustain more Bicycle Empowerment Centers, train staff and start the process of empowering communities across Namibia.

GiveNow is our secure online donation facility

Please consider donating funds directly to our projects through the commission-free online donations service GiveNow. Funds raised will be used to pay for shipping and clearance costs of sending bikes to Africa, ground transport in Australia and Africa, bike collections, containers and organisational administration. The bikes change lives forever by improving basic mobility for access to health care and education, and increasing economic productivity. The shipping container becomes a pre-built bicycle workshop providing further employment opportunities. You can donate through our secure online GiveNow facility. GiveNow is powered by Westpac and administered by OurCommunity. If you want to take it a step further consider Fundraising with us. Please note all donations are not tax deductible. We are currently working toward Deductible Gift Register status with the Australian Tax Office. However, we are a registered fundraising charity with the NSW department of Fair Trading and an incorporated association registered in NSW, Australia as Bicycles for Humanity, Sydney (INC9892018).
