No Kids or Racing bike donations thanks. We try to only send those bikes that are economically useful and fat tyre bikes work best with the sand and thorns in Africa. Thanks

The address for Sydney drop off is The St Ives Showgrounds, Mova Vale Rd. Zoom in or out Using + or _ on the Google map below to for a more detailed or broader view.

At this site we've partnered with Kuringai Council who are generously providing space and much more. This is where we prepare, collect bikes and load them into our next shipping container ready for Africa.

Meet us at the container and we'll receive your (mountain) bike, prepare it and pack it, ready for shipping.

Click here to get an idea of which bikes are best for African conditions.

Bikes for Humanity can transport your old bike to a Namibian community, establish a sustainable bike shop, and even train local staff. So while it may be worthless to you, that dusty two-wheeler could have a magnificent future improving access to health care, education and empowering people in Africa.
