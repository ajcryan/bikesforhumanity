No Kids or Racing bike donations thanks. We try to only send those bikes that are economically useful and fat tyre bikes work best with the sand and thorns in Africa. Thanks

St Ives is our only collection point in Sydney at this stage. We also have collection points in the Southern Highlands, or Canberra, or the Blue Mountains.

We're always looking for new spots to form bike collections so get in contact if you have ideas.

If you live far away, or can't make it on the day, and you need to get rid of a perfectly good bike, consider giving to one of the other organisations in the Sydney region who collect and recycle second hand bikes.
